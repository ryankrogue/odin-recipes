PB & J

Description

PB & J is a classic sandwich that anyone can make!

Ingredients

  • Sandwich Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter onto one of the pieces of bread
  3. Spread jelly onto the other piec of bread
  4. Put the two slices of bread on top of each other with the peanut butter and jelly sides facing inwards.
