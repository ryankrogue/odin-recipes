Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a super easy meal to make and is highly customizable to your own tastes. Today we'll be making a spaghetti with red sauce and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Dried Spaghetti Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water
  3. Simmer until Al Dente
  4. Chop the mushrooms
  5. Sautee the mushrooms
  6. Add marinara sauce to pan with mushrooms
  7. Serve the spaghetti with the sauce and mushrooms on top
